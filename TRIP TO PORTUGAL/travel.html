It all began on a Monday...





Even though we were very tired after such a long fly, we were excited to arrive and start this new adventure. The accommodation was very satisfying due to the brand-new houses where we stayed.



Let the game begin !





On Tuesday, we met our trainer, Ricardo, and we got the big picture of being a programmer. We were fascinated by all the possibilities we have in this domain. We've also seen the city and found its beauty.



Discovering Barcelos





On Friday, we had the afternoon off and due to the pleasant weather, we spent it outside, exploring the surroundings. We discovered an exhibition of sculptures and we've taken photos there.



First day off





This was the first day of the weekend and we've been to Porto where we tasted the excellent Offley wine. Also, we went on a boat. It was a bit cold but we've made some great memories.



What's up Braga ?





This day was awesome from all the points of view: the weather, the places we visited and the atmosphere. The Guimaraes Castle was like taken from a fairy tale and the gardens around it were all green and full of flowers. We loved this day the most !



Santiago





On the first day of our second weekend here, we visited the cathedral from Santiago de Compostella - Spain which is a breath-taking place, filled with mistery and peace. The architecture of the cathedral is incredible and we were amazed by how long it took for this place to be build.



Ahoy, Sailor !





Today was our last day as tourists and we fully enjoyed it. We visited Ponto de Lima which has a breath-taking garden, the grandious cathedral of Santa Lucia and we saw the Atlantic Ocean in all its beauty. Because it was the last day, we were all a little bit nostalgic, but we had fun and for this we will all remember Portugal.
