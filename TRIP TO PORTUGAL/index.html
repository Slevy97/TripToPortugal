Long story short



What is this site about? Two people out of eighteen and a project for web pages developers. The fascinating thing is that even though our purpose was learning how to create the page you are on right now, we discovered something more: a little brand-new world. Neither one of us knew anything more about Portugal than what everyone knows:

  • it's part of EU,
  • it's close to the Atlantic Ocean
  • it looks like something added up to Spain

Nothing magical as you may observe...

But Portugal means so much more now! This country has a spirit we couldn't find in other countries, a story, an attitude that provokes you to relax, to enjoy every second and to forget about any worries. This is what Portugal means to us: a place to hide from everything. We also appreciate the good food, the architecture and the hospitality of its inhabitants.

Check out Portugal !!
